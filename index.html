What are we doing?

Over the course of one (very tiring) week, we will attempt to climb the height of Mount Everest above sea level; 8,848 m or over 29,000 ft. We will do this by climbing up the stairs of Manchester's Arndale Tower, each time to a height of around 80m, in a stair relay between the team. In order to reach our target we will need to climb the tower a whopping 110 times.

Why are we doing this?

We wanted to help raise money for Comic Relief, but it to be needed a challenge at the same time; the result was the idea to climb Everest. Unfortunately, flights to Nepal weren't in our budget, so we decided to climb the nearest thing we had; the Arndale Tower.
